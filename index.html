Professional Pest Management Services for Commercial and Residential Customers

We understand that bugs, rodents, and other pests can be a real problem for your home or business. Not only do you not want to see them in your personal space, but they can also cause some severe damage to your property. Don’t let pests bug you any longer!

Century Pest Control delivers proven solutions to the toughest pest and wildlife issues. Protect your home. Safeguard your business. Get the peace of mind you need to ensure the safety of your family, pets, employees, customers, and property.

We Don’t Just Know the Business. We Love What We Do.

Professional Team

Our technicians are licensed and certified by the Texas Department of Agriculture. They are rigorously trained to ensure we deliver safe, quality pest management that works.

Safe Products

We only use materials that have been registered by the Environmental Protection Agency. Our line of materials is organically formulated, environmentally friendly, and safe.

Invested in the Industry

Century Pest Control is a member of the Local, State, and National Pest Control Association. We stay up-to-date on the latest industry standards so we can continue to deliver the best to our customers.

Family-Owned Business

The Angelini family has been serving San Antonio and the surrounding areas for 45 years. They focus on quality, dependable service from a team you can trust. It’s not just a business; it’s a passion.

Practical Solutions for Any Problem

Pest Control

From Ants and Mosquitoes to Termites and Cockroaches, we’re here to tackle all your pest problems. Stop letting those pests stress you! Our team will create an effective pest control program that caters to your needs.

Residential Service

Whether you need instant relief or annual preventative treatments, we’re here to keep your family and your home safe. We offer one-time services or yearly maintenance treatments to manage any pest problem you have at home.

Commercial Service

We help hundreds of businesses treat their commercial property. We’ll help you address common issues to keep your business or rental property clear of pest damage. We’ll even provide onsite documentation for your needs.

Rodents & Wildlife
